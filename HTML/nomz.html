The Purpose

The The Neighbor Network was created as a neighbor business platform, where neighbors can search for each other's services based on their profession. Taking in a Google Geo-Code as a parameter, it allows you to find neighbors that live next to you, and inquire about their services. The Neighbor Network was built in hopes that neighbors utilize each other's professions out of convenience. The unique rating system that was developed is one of the key components that helps insure that the consumer receives great service.
